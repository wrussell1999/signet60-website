In 1963, 4 SigneTs crossed the Channel - Just to prove they could!

60 years ago, a small convoy of sturdy little dinghies set out to disprove the myth around seaworthiness of SigneTs

This year the SigneT Class Owners Association will be completing a tribute to this voyage in aid of RNLI and Ellen MacArthur Cancer Trust!

When: 10th June 2022

Where: Dover to Calais
